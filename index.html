Features

◎

Aimbot

  • smooth camera aimbot, movement stays free while aiming
  • FOV circle: smooth, dots, or filled color picker
  • velocity prediction + line-of-sight checks
  • hit part select (head / hrp / torso), hold or toggle
  • smart target validation: skips dead, ragdoll, underground
⊹

Triggerbot

  • auto-fires when your aim is on an enemy
  • adjustable fire delay and trigger FOV
  • works off mouse target or the aimbot lock
⛶

Hitbox Expander new

  • expands enemy hitboxes for easier hits
  • team check, skips your own teammates
  • size slider + invisible mode with transparency
▣

ESP

  • corner or 3D bounding boxes
  • chams fill
  • names + equipped item
  • health bar (green → red gradient)
  • distance
  • tracers, 4 origins
  • skeleton (R6 + R15)
  • head dot
  • offscreen arrows
  • self ESP
  • 8 color presets + picker
  • custom font + size
✛

Crosshair

  • 6 styles: cross, dot, circle, X, KV, sniper
  • size, gap, thickness, opacity
  • dynamic spread, rainbow, outline, center dot
◴

Radar

  • circular compass radar (N / E / S / W)
  • enemy + team colors, health-tinted dots
  • range slider, off-radar edge arrows, names
⚡

Movement

  • speed (walkspeed / cframe / velocity)
  • fly + shift boost, float, bunny hop
  • noclip, jump power
✦

World & Combat

  • fullbright, no fog, custom FOV
  • gun mods: fast reload, fire rate, auto
  • no spread, no recoil
⚙

Misc & QoL

  • anti-afk, auto rejoin, anti-fling
  • auto tp, server hop, chat spam / spy
  • watermark (fps / ping / time), panic key
  • custom keybinds + notification sounds
